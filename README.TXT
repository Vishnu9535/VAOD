VAOD - Voice Assisted Object_Detection for Visully impared

VAODfiles contains different files related to the model
    audio.py gives the audio output 
    derection.py gives the direction of object 
    model.py is the implimentation of the Mobilenetssd model
    object_size.py gives the sizes of the objects detected in the frame 
    distance.py gives the distance of object from the camera

videos folder consist of few sample videos 

VAOD.py the implimets the final object_detection model  which gives
direction as well as distance of the objects detected from the camera input

Run VAOD.py
